## Tom Williams

I’m a computational evolutionary biologist interested in the history of life and the processes of evolution. I mainly work on questions about the “tree of life”, evolution in deep time, and the evolution of (usually microbial) genomes, from the perspectives of phylogenetics, comparative genomics and bioinformatics. Some of the questions I work on include:

- Reconstructing the "tree of life"
- The origin of eukaryotic cells
- Early cell evolution
- The course of metabolic evolution
- Co-evolutionary relationships, including symbiosis and endosymbiosis
- Phylogenetics and bioinformatics methods development
